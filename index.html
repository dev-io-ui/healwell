About Us

2018

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2018, HealWell Clinic began with a vision to offer affordable and accessible healthcare to the community. We started as a small clinic with a dedicated team of two doctors and a nurse.

2020

Expanding Services

In 2020, we expanded our services to include pediatrics, physiotherapy, and diagnostics, aiming to provide comprehensive care under one roof with the help of specialized professionals.

2022

Modern Facilities

We upgraded our infrastructure with modern diagnostic equipment and digital health record systems to enhance patient experience, reduce wait times, and improve treatment accuracy.

2025

Trusted Community Care

Today,HealWell Clinic is a trusted name in the region, serving thousands of patients annually with a focus on compassionate, preventive, and personalized care. We continue to grow with your trust.

Our Services

General Consultation

Comprehensive health check-ups and expert medical advice for all age groups, ensuring timely diagnosis and care for a wide range of conditions.

Pathology & Diagnostics

Advanced laboratory testing, including blood tests, urine analysis, ECG, and more, with quick and accurate results to assist in effective treatment.

Women's Health

Specialized care including gynecology consultations, prenatal/postnatal care, and preventive screenings tailored to women’s wellness.

Emergency Care

Immediate response for urgent medical needs with our on-call doctors and first-aid-equipped facilities available during critical hours.
